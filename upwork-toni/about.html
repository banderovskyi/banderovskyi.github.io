Get to know me

Hi - Im Toni. Im a failed NBA player turned Fullstack growth marketer. I make the internet fun at Figma during the day, and spend my nights and weekends shipping projects that interest me like NBAColors, Spreadbot, and WagerAPI.

I grew up in sunny South Florida, the son of Cuban and Lebanese immigrants. I prefer tropical climate & latin jazz, but these days you can find me eating Meatball subs in San Francisco.

This site serves as part portfolio, part outlet for my 1000 hobbies. Right now Im really into pre-fab architecture, Stellar, wood working, film photography and motorcycle restoration.

Fun fact about me, I was in an Asana ad!
